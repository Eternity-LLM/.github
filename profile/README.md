# Eternity-LLM Organization

[![Eternity-LLM Organization](https://eternity-llm.github.io/icons/icon_text.svg)](https://eternity-llm.github.io/)

Welcome to Eternity-LLM Organization, a dedicated research collective focused on pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence through the development of high-performance, open-source large language models (LLMs) and the pursuit of safe, beneficial Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).

Our mission is to advance the field of artificial intelligence (AI) by creating robust, scalable, and efficient models, fostering open collaboration, and contributing to the global research community. We believe in transparent and accessible AI development to drive innovation for everyone.

Explore our repositories, contribute to our projects, and join us on our journey to shape the future of intelligence.
